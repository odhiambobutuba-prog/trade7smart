Auto AI — 1-minute candle engine

Auto AI watches the live server-timestamped tick clock for the selected market. When the countdown reaches your trigger second inside each 1-minute candle, it requests a fresh price and buys automatically for the configured number of ticks. Nothing here is simulated — every buy is a real contract on your connected account.
